Story behind Lukenya Getaway

The Lukenya Getaway Limited began in 2001 as a countryside home. Our serene environment and perfect location resulted in past returning here seeking a home away from home experience. As demand increased, we set up our Cottages to accommodate our former and new guests. From a humble beginning as a countryside home, we have grown over the years to offer a wide range of services and facilities such as a pool, a spacious dining area, and a volleyball pitch

What services do you offer?

At Lukenya Getaway Limited, we offer you a home away from home in a serene environment. Our services include hotel, accommodation, conferencing, camping, team building activities, and other fun activities.

What are your rates?

Our rates differ depending on the services that you choose. For more information or to enquire about a customized package, kindly reach out to us either by email or by phone call.

What is your accommodation availability?

Our accommodation is open all year round. Availability depends on the season of the year. To find out about availability and to make a booking, kindly contact us on our email or via phone call.

Yes. We do understand that things can change and offer flexibility to all our current and future customers. Our cancellation policy is as follows: ● 35 to 20 days- 10% ● 19 to 10 days – 25% ● 9 to 2 days – 35% ● 2 to 0 days – 100%.

What Payment methods do you accept?

At Lukenya Getaway, we accept all major credit cards as well as MPesa as a form of payment.
