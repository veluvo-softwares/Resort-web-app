Corporate Events

The Lukenya Getaway Limited began in 2001 as a countryside home. Our serene environment and perfect location resulted in past returning here seeking a home away from home experience. As demand increased, we set up our Cottages to accommodate our former and new guests. From a humble beginning as a countryside home, we have grown over the years to offer a wide range of services and facilities such as a pool, a spacious dining area, and a volleyball pitch
